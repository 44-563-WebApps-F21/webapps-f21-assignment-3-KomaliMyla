RUBY MORGAN

Ruby Morghan is a 1000 year old Hybrid who is both Vampire and a Werewolf.Ruby was born a werewolf but her mother turns her into a vampire to protecty her from all the danger in the world.
But as it turns out Ruby has now become the danger to the world by unleashing her dark nature. By creating a werewolf army with an insane ritual she became the biggest threat that walked this earth.
